Admission Procedure

Admission Procedures And Fees

At Cambridge school, the admission procedure begins at the start of every academic year i.e. January. The admission is solely based on merit.

For KG to grade I, the admission is subject to vacancies or availability of seats and are based on written tests which are often followed by an interactive session. The application forms must be duly filled in, signed and submitted within 3 days of the date of collection of forms and within the last prescribed date. The application must be accompanied by three passport sized photographs and an attested copy of the birth certificate, original school leaving certificate as well as other necessary documents needed for the admission procedure.

The school collects nominal fees catering to all strata of society. The school believes that each child has the right to education. Tuition fees are collected every month and are inclusive of food, transportation and other activities in school.
