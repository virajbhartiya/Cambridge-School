Curriculm

Cambridge School, Aurangabad offers education from kindergarten to grade XI under the ICSE and ISC boards. The subjects provided in the primary sections are English, Science, Maths. Hindi, Computer, Social Studies, Environmental Education and Marathi as their compulsory third language. In the secondary section, English, Science, Maths, Hindi, Computer studies, Social Studies, Environmental Education are compulsory till grade VIII and from grade IX, English, Hindi and Social Studies (History, Civic, and Geography) are compulsory with two others subjects to be chosen from Maths, Science, Economics and Commercial Studies and one between Computer Applications and Home Science. For grade XI, students are offered subjects compatible with the Commerce stream.

EXTRA-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

At Cambridge School, Students also have the option to choose from a vast variety of activities to complement their interests and hone their individual talents. The activities can be broadly categorized into Sports, Music, Dance, Drama and Arts.

OTHER EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES INCLUDE

SPORTS

All the physical activities are conducted on their respective playgrounds and courts. The students are supervised and guided by trained sports coaches. DANCE

The dance section has a spacious mirrored dance hall with wooden flooring and trained dance instructors. The students are taught traditional, western as well as fusion forms of dance. In addition, the students are also taught aerobics to help them remain fit and agile throughout the dance session.

ART

The students of Cambridge school are trained by qualified art teachers. They are instructed with the basics of sketching, drawing, collage making, colouring, painting, model making, and many other art techniques. Exposure to art allows our students to express themselves. The school also teaches the students through hands-on experience in project making and handicrafts.
