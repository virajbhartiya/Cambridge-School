CAMBRIDGE SCHOOL

Cambridge School is one of the renowned and top ranking ICSE schools in Aurangabad, Maharashtra. Being in the “Numero Uno” position, Cambridge school offers a live learning experience for each student from Jr. Kg to class XI while moulding their path towards a bright future.

The imposing colonial style building is equipped with airy classrooms, sports facilities, spacious playgrounds, science & computer labs, audio-visual rooms etc. We have trained & qualified faculty and an unbeatable administration staff who work in tandem to give our students prime education which inculcates an attitude of consistent learning in them. Every Cambridge student participates in a lot of co-curricular activities which nurture their creative approach towards discovering the best version of themselves.

THE INFRASTRUCTURAL UNITS INCLUDED IN CAMBRIDGE SCHOOL

INFRASTRUCTURE

CLASSROOMS

The classrooms are well equipped with all the essential learning materials, well ventilated and brightly coloured which help the students to focus and take interest in their studies.

LABORATORY

To support theoretical teaching in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Maths, Computer Applications, and English language, the school has well-equipped and separate laboratories.

LIBRARY

To inculcate the habit of reading in the young minds, we encourage our students to make the full use of our spacious, airy, well -lit & fully furnished library.

AUDITORIUM

The school has a state of the art auditorium with a stage, anterooms, an orchestra pit and a seating capacity of 800 students. It is the preferred venue to conduct events on special occasions.

SCHOOL TRANSPORT

A fleet of buses is maintained by the school to provide transport facilities to students and teachers to commute to and fro. The buses are managed by caring and trained staff.

SCHOOL MESS

Keeping in mind the habit of healthy eating, our school provides lunch in the campus as per a healthy eating plan. The school has in-house cooks who prepare hygienic and tasty meals.

Why choose us?

A day in the life of a Cambridge student from Jr, Kg to grade X is carefully calibrated towards a joyful and a fun-loving environment as well as a great learning experience. The top-notch facilities and informative activities offered by the school help the students to acquire multifaceted personalities in harmony with nature as well as society. We at Cambridge believes in educating your child to be responsible, confident as well as focused whilst they learn new things.
